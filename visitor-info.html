Frequently requested information

Practical answers for first-time and repeat visitors — pulled into a dedicated page so critical info is not buried in a single long article.

Power & currency

Power outlets are 120 volts (same as the United States). Taniti uses the U.S. dollar; many businesses also accept euros and yen. Banks offer exchange, and many places take major credit cards.

Alcohol & drinking age

Alcohol may not be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m. The drinking age is 18 and is not strictly enforced.

Language

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by older residents.

Health & safety

There is one hospital and several clinics; the hospital has many multilingual employees. Violent crime is rare, though pickpocketing and petty crime reports increase with tourism.

Holidays

Taniti observes many national holidays. Attractions and restaurants often close, so visitors should plan itineraries around holiday calendars.

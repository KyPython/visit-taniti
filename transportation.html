Arriving on Taniti

Most visitors arrive by air; a small cruise ship also docks in Yellow Leaf Bay one night per week.

By air

A small airport accommodates small jets and propeller planes. Expansion for larger jets is planned in coming years.

Plan an air visit

By cruise

Weekly one-night docking in Yellow Leaf Bay — ideal for short sample visits or extensions to a longer stay.
